# OBJECTIVES: Seismic Physics

**Module:** `universe/seismic_physics`
**Question:** What does the seismic physics simulation optimize as a Cities of Light universe?

---

## O1: Realistic seismic simulation as citizen experience substrate

The seismic physics engine is the world-state layer that every citizen inhabits. It is not a visual effect or a narrative annotation -- it is the continuous physical reality of Contre-Terre. Every tick, the world shakes. Every citizen body responds. The simulation must produce a magnitude field that citizens experience as their ground truth: background vibration at magnitude 4-5 is their silence, their heartbeat, the pulse they were born into. Anything below 4 is meaningless. Anything above 6 is an event.

**What this optimizes:** A world where seismic state is not optional flavor but the foundational layer on which Contact, tremens, architecture, and survival all depend. Citizens do not "observe" earthquakes -- they live inside a permanently vibrating field, and the physics engine produces that field tick by tick.

---

## O2: Tremens as citizen internal state

The tremens is the physiological response of a citizen body to frequency zones different from its natal calibration. It is not a status effect applied externally -- it emerges from the distance between the citizen's natal seismic spectrum and the current zone's spectrum. The physics engine must compute tremens per citizen per tick: a citizen born in the high-frequency archipelago cliffs who descends into the low-frequency volcanic interior will suffer escalating tremens (nausea, trembling, hallucinations, incapacitation). A citizen in their natal zone feels nothing.

**What this optimizes:** Interiority. The world changes inside the citizen, not just around them. The tremens makes geography personal -- two citizens standing in the same zone experience different tremens intensities depending on where they were born. This is the mechanism that transforms physical descent into physiological transformation.

---

## O3: Magnitude as world state driving narrative moments

The global magnitude field is a world-level variable that evolves over time. Background magnitude sits at 4-5 permanently. Seismes are discrete events (magnitude 5-8+) generated probabilistically from accumulated tectonic tension. The magnitude 11 is the horizon event -- never measured by instruments, only felt by predicteurs. The physics engine must produce magnitude as a continuous field with discrete events layered on top, so that narrative moments (deaths, separations, Contact degradation) emerge from physical state rather than being scripted.

**What this optimizes:** Narrative emergence from physics. Seismes are not plot points inserted by an author -- they are events generated by the tension accumulation algorithm. When a seisme kills a citizen, it is because the physics produced that magnitude at that tick in that zone. The story follows the physics, not the reverse.

---

## O4: Frequency zones as geographically coherent regions

The world is divided into frequency zones, each with a distinct seismic spectrum. The surface desert has low, slow, deep waves ("an animal breathing in its sleep"). The intermediate piedmont has dry, nervous, clacking frequencies. The deep caverns have infra-bass vibrations below conscious perception. The volcanic interior has deformed, heat-warped waves. Each zone must have a coherent spectral signature that triggers tremens transitions in citizens crossing zone boundaries.

**What this optimizes:** Geographic identity through physics. A citizen does not need a map to know which zone they are in -- their body tells them. The frequency change triggers tremens, which is the body's way of saying "this place is not home." Zone transitions are physiological events, not map annotations.

---

## O5: The building 11 as systemic threat horizon

The magnitude 11 is not a parameter in the normal range. It is the catastrophic event that the entire mission exists to pre-trigger. The physics engine must model the building 11 as an accumulation of tectonic tension that increases monotonically over the novel's timeline -- intervals between precursors shorten, deep harmonics intensify, predicteur citizens sense the approach through worsening tremens. The 11 never appears on instruments. It lives only in the bodies of those calibrated to feel it.

**What this optimizes:** Systemic dread. The physics engine itself knows the 11 is coming (it is the tension variable approaching threshold), but the simulated instruments cannot measure it. Only predicteur citizens, through their tremens, produce the warning signal. The gap between what physics knows and what instruments report IS the epistemological conflict of the novel.

---

## HIERARCHY

```
O1 (world substrate) > O3 (magnitude drives narrative) > O2 (tremens per citizen) > O4 (frequency zones) > O5 (building 11)
```

**Principal tradeoff:** If the physics engine optimizes for spectacle (big earthquakes, dramatic events), it eclipses the tremens and Contact systems that carry the human experience. If it optimizes for subtlety (quiet vibrations, slow changes), it fails to produce the escalation that drives narrative tension. The balance: background magnitude is permanent and quiet (the world's heartbeat), while discrete seismes are violent and rare enough to be events. The escalation comes from frequency, not from individual magnitude -- seismes happen more often as tension builds, until the 11 breaks the pattern entirely.
